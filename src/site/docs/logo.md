## Style

SemBeacon uses a logo with the color #363795.

The logo represents a combination of the Eddystone logo (square), the [AltBeacon](https://altbeacon.org) logo and the logo of the [Vrije Universiteit Brussel](https://vub.be/)
where the specification was originally designed.
