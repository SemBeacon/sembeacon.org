Logo

Style and Guidelines

SemBeacon uses a logo with the color #363795. On dark backgrounds the logo is displayed in white. The logo can be displayed with or without the SemBeacon name. However, the logo should always include the pentagon-shaped logo.

Whenever possible, the logo should be displayed using vector graphics.

Meaning

The logo represents a combination of the Eddystone logo (square with a cut out "Epsilon"), the AltBeacon logo with its hexagon shape and the logo of the Vrije Universiteit Brussel where the specification was designed.
